About Me

I am a graduate student at Johns Hopkins University's Whiting School of Engineering. My research interests are intrepretability of deep neural networks, universal domain adaptation, and cross-lingual natural language processing, especially with regards to low-resource languages. In the main, my goal is to support the development of trustworthy Artificial Intelligence and dissolve online language barriers to make the internet equally available to people from all corners of the globe. I also enjoy learning human languages and have studied English, Hindi, Urdu, French, and Italian.
